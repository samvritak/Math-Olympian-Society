Tips for Instructors

This page is filled with tips for instructors/PICO's. PICO stands for Person In Charge of Olympiads. We hope you find this useful!

Goals
  • Deepening their enthusiasm for mathematics and problem solving
  • Providing students with practice material for the tournaments
  • Having weekly classes
  • Giving students an enriching experience
Practice Material
  • Syllabus can be found here
  • Weekly slides can be found here
  • Additional resources, including practice tests and books can be found here
Classes
  • Preferably have one class per week and cover one topic
  • Every week before test week let the students take a practice test to prepare
  • Provide additional homework or other practice resources to deepen understanding of a concept
  • Whether you take online or in person classes, make sure that everyone participates actively in activities and assignments
